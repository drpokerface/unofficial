Introducing Virtualgold

Virtualgold is a digital asset that is stored on the ethereum blockchain. It uses smart contract technology to ensure complete transparency, security and decentralized ownership. But unlike other standard cryptocurrencies whose value is determined by supply and demand, virtual gold utilizes a very unique and innovative model to sustainably increase its value. At a very fundamental level virtualgold has been created in such a way that its price can never reduce, it can only grow. The rate of increase however may fluctuate based upon demand.






How does the system work?

66.6% of the ETH you invest would be converted to virtualgold at the conversion rate. The remaining ETH is used as a virtualgold royalty fee. The main reason why this fee is being charged is for boosting the price of virtualgold. Whenever a new person invests into the system a portion of their investment is paid out to all virtualgold holders in proportion to the size of their holdings. This money is indirectly paid to them by increasing the amount of ETH backing the virtualgold price within the smart contract. This is what causes the price of virtualgold to increase after every investment. There is no upper limit your profits! The longer you hold on to your virtualgold the greater would be your return on investment. Your virtualgold and the corresponding value in ETH backing its value is always secured within the smart contract. Also, 66.6% of your investment always remains untouched. This is done to reduce risk on investment. So under no circumstance even if you are the last person to join the system you cannot lose more than 33.3% of your investment.

If others sell their virtualgold does the price drop?

In short the answer is no. At a very fundamental level virtualgold is different from other blockchain assets. The price is not dictated by supply and demand. Once people buy virtualgold there is a certain amount of ETH backing its value within the smart contract. As new people join, a portion of their investment is distributed to all holders indirectly by increasing the amount of ETH backing the price of virtualgold. This way for the amount of virtualgold you own, it's equivalent value in ETH is always secured in the smart contract. Assume the current price for 1gram of virtual gold is $100 dollars and you own 2grams. Even if everyone sells their holdings $200 will still be left within the contract exclusively for you. This value can never go down as people sell. But it would increase as more and more people buy.

Can you transfer virtual gold to another address?

Nope it is not possible. Once you buy virtualgold your holdings are uniquely linked to your wallet, and stored in the virtualgold smart contract on the ethereum blockchain. You wouldn't be receiving any tokens in your wallet. Therefore it cannot be transferred. Virtualgold is not a traditional cryptocurrency used for facilitating transactions. The main motivation behind virtualgold was to create a digital asset solely for investors. Virtualgold is more like the blockchain equivalent of a real world appreciating asset like land or gold where investors buy in order to sell at a higher price later.

Are early investors rewarded more than newer ones?

Although everybody's investment grows with time, the rate of growth of of virtualgold is inversely proportionate to the number of virtual gold holders. Therefore those investors who invest when there are fewer holders(when there is lesser money in the smart contact), would observe a quicker growth in investment. Therefore it would be more lucrative to invest early when there are not many holders.
