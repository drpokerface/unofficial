Introducing VirtualGold

Virtual gold is a digital asset that is stored on the ethereum blockchain. It uses smart contract technology to ensure complete transparency, security and decentralized ownership. But unlike other standard cryptocurrencies whose value is determined by supply and demand, virtual gold utilizes a very unique and innovative model to sustainably increase its value. At a very fundamental level virtual gold has been programmed in such a way that its price can never reduce, it can only grow. The rate of increase may fluctuate based upon demand. Early investments would observe greater and quicker growth as compared to subsequent ones.







How does the system work?

66% of the ethereum you invest will be converted to virtual gold at the conversion rate. The remaining ethereum is used for virtual gold mining fees. The main reason why this fee is being charged is for boosting the price of virtual gold. Whenever a new person invests into the system a portion of their investment is paid out to all virtual gold holders in proportion to the size of their holdings. This money is indirectly paid to them by increasing the amount of ethereum backing their virtual gold within the smart contract. This is what causes the price of virtual gold to increase after every investment. There is no upper limit your profits! The longer you hold on to your virtual gold the greater would be your return on investment. Your virtual gold and the corresponding value in ethereum backing its value is always secured within the smart contract. Also, 66% of your investment always remains untouched. This is done to reduce risk on investment. So under no circumstance even if you are the last person to join the system you cannot lose more than 33% of your investment.







Security

Virtual gold uses smart contract technology to ensure complete transparency, security and decentralized ownership. All the code in the smart contract is publicly viewable. Therefore any form of scam or malpractice is impossible.

Why is USDT used instead of ethereum?

The main reason why we use usdt to back the virtual gold in the smart contract is because usdt is a stable coin pegged to us dollar. If we had build the system using ethereum or some other cryptocurrency even if you cash out 10x what you invested it may not be worth 10x due to fluctuations in the value of non-stable cryptocurrencies. Therefore in order to make the project a long and sustainable project, we felt it would be wiser to use the most popular stable cryptocurrency-usdt.

Can you transfer virtual gold to another address?

Nope it is not possible. Once you buy virtual gold your holdings are uniquely linked only to your wallet, until you chose to cash out(aka sell). It cannot be transferred. Virtual gold is not a traditional cryptocurrency used for facilitating transactions. The main motivation behind virtual gold was to create a digital asset solely for investors. Virtual gold is more like the blockchain equivalent of a real world appreciating asset like land or gold where investors buy in order to sell at a higher price later.

Are early investors rewarded more than newer ones?

Although everybody's investment grows with time, the rate of growth of of virtual gold is inversely proportionate to the number of virtual gold holders. Therefore those investors who invest when there are fewer holders(when there is lesser money in the smart contact), would observe a quicker growth in investment. Therefore the earlier the investment the more lucrative it would be.
